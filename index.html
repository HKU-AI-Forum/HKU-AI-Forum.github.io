About This Workshop

This workshop aims to bring together world-leading AI researchers and rising stars, providing a platform for global scholars to exchange ideas and inspiration. Topics include but not limited to: generative models, multimodal AI, embodied AI, computer vision, machine learning and deep learning theory. This event is jointly organized by HKU Musketeers Foundation Institute of Data Science (HKU IDS) and Artificial Intelligence Lab. The previous sessions were successfully held on December 6-7, 2023 and April 28-29, 2024.
